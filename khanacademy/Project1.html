Discover Seoul

Seoul, the capital of South Korea, is a city where ancient traditions meet modern technology. From historic palaces to neon-lit streets, Seoul offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

Places to Visit

  • Gyeongbokgung Palace
  • N Seoul Tower
  • Bukchon Hanok Village
  • Myeongdong Shopping Street
  • Hongdae

Things to Try

  • Kimchi and Korean BBQ
  • Street food at night markets
  • Traditional Korean tea
  • Hanbok experience

Whether you're walking through peaceful palaces or enjoying vibrant nightlife, Seoul will captivate you with its energy, culture, and food.
